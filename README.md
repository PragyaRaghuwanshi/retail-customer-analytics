# Retail Customer Analytics

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail customer purchasing behavior, demographic characteristics, product spending patterns, and marketing campaign performance using Excel, SQL, Python, and Tableau.

The goal was to transform raw customer data into actionable business insights that support customer segmentation, campaign optimization, product strategy, and data-driven decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Retail businesses often face challenges in understanding:

* Which customers are most likely to respond to marketing campaigns
* Which product categories generate the highest revenue
* How customer demographics influence purchasing behavior
* Which sales channels customers prefer
* How customer engagement changes over time

This project addresses these challenges through data analysis and interactive visualization.

## Key Business Insights

* Graduation customers represent the largest customer segment.
* Customers aged 56+ show the highest campaign engagement.
* Wine products generate the highest customer spending.
* Store purchases remain the dominant purchasing channel.
* Higher-income customers tend to spend more and respond more positively to campaigns.
* Marketing campaign response rates remain relatively low, highlighting opportunities for improved targeting.
